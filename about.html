Our Story

Started by betting on herself

Perfect Trim Lawn Care is owned and run by Octavia — a country girl who decided to bet on herself, leave her job, and go all in on lawn care. What started as one woman with a mower and a high standard has grown into a trusted, insured, woman- & veteran-owned business serving Montgomery and the River Region.

That standard hasn't changed. Every yard gets the same detail: clean cuts, crisp edges, tidy beds and a full clean-up before we leave. We treat your property like it's our own — because our name is on it.

When you hire Perfect Trim, you're supporting a local small business that genuinely cares how your lawn turns out. And it shows.
